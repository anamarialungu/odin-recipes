Valentine's Day French Toast

Description

Surprise your significant other with this delicious breakfast on Valentine's Day morning and be sure you will win their heart once again.

Preparation time: 10 mins. Cooking time: 15 mins. Total: 25 mins. This recipe is suitable for 2 servings.

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 3 dashes ground nutmeg
  • 2 dashes ground cinnamon
  • 4 slices bread
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 6 strawberries, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup, warmed
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Beat eggs, milk, nutmeg, and cinnamon together with a fork in a bowl until somewhat smooth.
  2. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Dip a bread slice into egg mixture to evenly and generously coat each side and put into the hot skillet; cook until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Repeat with each bread slice.
  4. Cut each slice in half on a diagonal and arrange onto 2 plates. Arrange banana and strawberry slices on the plates around the French toast. Drizzle maple syrup over the French toast; dust with confectioners' sugar.
